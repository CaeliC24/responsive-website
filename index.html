What Is NeuroKare?

NeuroKare is a medical clinic that combines personalized medication management with structured daily guidance. Our approach is designed to remove guesswork and help patients understand what to focus on each day, while remaining supported by a dual-board certified licensed medical provider.

The NeuroReset Program reflects our belief that sustainable improvement happens when medication, daily habits, and nervous system regulation are addressed together.

The Role of Low Dose Naltrexone

When clinically appropriate, Low Dose Naltrexone (LDN) will be incorporated as a component of the NeuroReset Program.

Supportive, Not Standalone

LDN is used as a supportive tool within a broader, structured recovery program.

Explored Across Conditions

LDN has been explored across a wide range of symptoms and conditions, with side effects generally considered mild when appropriately prescribed.

Carefully Monitored

Any medication use is closely monitored and adjusted based on individual response.
